## Two Ways (bidirectional integration)

Bridge provides bidirectional integration to push and recieve contact related events from your CRM to Bridge and biseversa:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="REST API" icon="code" href="/api-reference">

    CRUD operations via API REST
  </Card>
